1) Click "Debug F580_PCA1_Capture_Input ..." button in the toolbar.
2) Click Resume in the toolbar (or press F8).
3) Press the button twice in less than 4 seconds.
4) Click Suspend in the toolbar.
5) Click on the Expressions view. Add the new expression "BUTTON_PRESS_DELAY" (without quotes). The value of that expression is the number of cycles between button presses.
